Instructions

Single Character Simulator

  1. Start by choosing the card you wish to run the simulation on.
    • Use the auto button to quickly select the current most likely best settings for the character and move to step 5 OR follow the instructions below.
  2. Choose from the common leader skills you want to be applied during the simulation. You can choose to have none or just 1 apply.
  3. Choose the Super Attack level for the chosen character.
  4. Check the boxes that represent the hidden potential system.
    • Checking "Rainbow" will override all the other inputs and will be the same as selecting all the individual branches.
    • Checking a branch will assume you want the entirety of the branch, including what would be available without a dupe (the free dupe area).
  5. Enter a number for how many times you want to run the simulator. Choosing a higher number will give a more accurate picture on the average attack of the unit. The single card sim is limited to a maximum of 1000 turns.
  6. Once everything is ready, press the run simulation button.
  7. When the simulation has finished, the results will be displayed underneath?.
